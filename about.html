Welcome to the STARS Lab! We explore and develop intelligent systems that are autonomous and resilient in various socio-technical settings. Our projects are generously supported by National Science Foundation, NVIDIA, FedEx Institute of Technology, and University of Memphis.


We are a highly interdisciplinary team and have a wide range of research interests. Our current research topics include the following.

  • City-scale traffic simulation and reconstruction
  • Next-gen intelligent transportation systems and smart cities
  • Multi-agent reinforcement learning and data-driven control
  • Adversarial machine learning for autonomous and resilient systems
  • Network science and complex systems

The STARS Lab is directed by Prof. Weizi Li in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Memphis. Feel free to explore our research, find out what happened recently, and get in touch!
